## Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Background:** `#2b2b2b` (Dark gray)
- **Primary:** `#4a9eff` (Blue)
- **Accent:** `#d32f2f` (Red - logo)

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#ffffff` (White)
- **Secondary Text:** `#cccccc` (Light gray)
- **Muted Text:** `#999999` (Gray)
- **Highlighted Text:** `#b3d4ff` (Light blue)

### Component Colors
- **Card Background:** `#333333` (Medium gray)
- **Border:** `#444444` (Dark gray)
- **Gradient Start:** `#1e3c72` (Dark blue)
- **Gradient End:** `#2a5298` (Medium blue)
- **Button Gradient:** `#4a9eff` to `#357abd`

## Responsive Design

Both templates are designed to be:
- **Mobile-friendly:** Readable on small screens
- **Email client compatible:** Table-based layout
- **Accessible:** Plain text fallback included
- **Professional:** Consistent branding throughout

## Customization

### Easy Customization Points

1. **Company Logo**
   - Update URL in templates
   - Current: `https://leviticatechnologies.com/assets/Levitica%20logo.png`

2. **Color Scheme**
   - Modify hex colors in templates
   - Maintain contrast for readability

3. **Content**
   - Update feature lists
   - Modify instructions
   - Change support contact info

4. **Links**
   - Mobile app download link
   - Web portal URL
   - Support email

5. **Branding**
   - Company name (passed dynamically)
   - Logo
   - Footer text
